Before you start a referral

To use this service, you need:

  • To understand the whole picture of this persons need requirements and be able to explain why you are making the referral
  • Consider doing a quick check to see what services a family may be eligible for
  • You must have the consent of the person or parent or guardian to refer them for a service
  • The details and contact details for the person or family being referred
  • To ensure nobody is in immediate danger
