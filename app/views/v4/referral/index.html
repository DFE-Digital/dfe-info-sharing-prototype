Request for early help and support for aged 0 to 5 targeted services

Refer a child aged 0 to 5 for care services that fall under the statutory care threshold.

Request early help and support for someone:

  • Aged 0 to 5 years old
  • When their needs fall under the satutory care threshold
  • When they are in no immdiate danger or have ongoing safeguarding concerns
  • If you are a professional and not a member of the general public

Before you start a referral

To use this service, you need:

  • Understanding of the child's needs
  • Consider doing a quick check to see what services a child may be eligible for
  • You must have the consent of the parent or guardian to refer the child for a targeted service
  • The details and contact details for the family being referred
  • To ensure the child is not in immediate danger
