Request for early help and support for aged 0 to 5 targeted services

Refer a child aged 0 to 5 for care services that fall under the statutory care threshold.

Request early help and support for a child:

  • aged 0 to 5 years old
  • if you are a professional working with the child
  • when their needs fall under the statutory care threshold
  • when they are not in immediate danger and there are no ongoing safeguarding concerns

Before you start a referral

To use this service, you need:

  • an understanding of the child's needs
  • to check what services the child may be eligible for
  • consent of the parent or guardian to refer the child for a targeted service
  • details and contact details for the child being referred
  • to ensure the child is not in immediate danger
