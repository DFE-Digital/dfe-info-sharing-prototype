Information you need before starting a request for early help and support for a child

The family

  • all family members and their contact details
  • consent from the parents and guardians

Other people

  • other significant people involved with the family such as grandparents or neighbours

Your details

  • your details of who you are, your relationship and contact

Reason for the request

  • issues the family are facing and how long they have been going on for
  • effects the issues are having
  • views of the family
  • family's desired outcomes
  • your desired outcomes

Family history

  • important information that needs to be taken into consideration when looking at the request for help
  • previous assessments that may have taken place

You will be given the opportunity to submit supporting documentation with the request.
