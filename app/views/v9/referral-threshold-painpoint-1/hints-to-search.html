Health visitors, GPs and school nurses are all examples of universal services as are leisure centres and parks in the community. These services are expected to make reasonable adjustments to ensure that children and young people with SEND have no barriers to accessing them.

Finding universal services

To find universal services:

  • Check on the family's local authority website for universal services available in the area
  • Do a search online for universal services in the area using a description of the child's needs
