Info sharing - Alpha Directory service designs

What is the directory service

For Professionals actively working with families within a local Authority who want to understand the full picture of a child’s previous interactions with services.

It is envisioned to save professionals time trying to identify previous touchpoints, and help professionals understand a child’s complete journey in order to provide the best support possible.

{{ govukTag({ text: "Current sprint" }) }} Sprint 1

Sprint goals

  • Establish a baseline for the type of data to be viewed on each child record
  • Establish a baseline for the type of data to be viewed on each professional record
  • Baseline for search and filter facilities within the design

What we learnt during this sprint

A high level flow has been established, this will form the basis of our various iterations, both for searching for child records as well as professional records.

The term 'family' has been deemed out of scope as it is currently difficult too define the makeup of a family. Instead, we are focussing efforts around the child's record and their touchpoints with professionals.

While the project aim is for a nationwide service, cross LA address tracking is considered a post MVS action at present.
