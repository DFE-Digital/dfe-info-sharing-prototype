Info sharing - Alpha Referral service designs

What the referral service is

The referral process is to allow professionals and front-line workers to refer a person or family in need for targeted services (social services). This service aims to help the workers in the community hubs by elevating the pain points within the referral process.

Sprint learnings

As a interaction designer for the referral service on the Info Sharing Alpha during sprint 1 I have been able to understand at a more detailed level..

  • The two specific flows required for the referrals process and eligibility checking process.
  • The minimal screens available in each flow that a user can go throught to achieve their goals of submitting a referral or completing an eligibility check.

Three generic flows have been defined and agreed to be within scope with the team.

Two of flows defined here, the referral form and eligibility checker are within scope for testing wthin the next sprint and the joined up process following through from eligibility checker to referral is not.
